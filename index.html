Architecture & Design

I am an architecture student investigating the spatial consequences of rapid urbanization — the erasure of rural identity, the commodification of historical memory, and the alienation embedded in contemporary labor environments.

My work translates critical theory into architectural proposals that make these tensions legible in space.
